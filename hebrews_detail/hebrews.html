    THE BOOK OF HEBREWS

    The book of Hebrews is a sublime book. Throughout the ages, it has been considered the most eloquent and one of the most important books of doctrine. It was written before AD 70 and is one of two New Testament books whose author is unknown. This book was written to Jewish believers and was given to help them in the transition between the Old Testament and New Testament.


    To all believers today, this text shows us the end that the Lord desires for each one of us, the way that we must take to reach this end, and a clear revelation of Christ as our High Priest.

    The study of this book will help us understand the spiritual truths behind what is expressed in the Old Testament.

    It will also help us understand the manner in which the New Testament fulfills the Old Testament in a more glorious way, as it is the fulfillment of the shadow, the reality of the Truth.

    PASTOR MARVIN BYERS

    Pastor Marvin Byers was born again in 1949. He studied Chemistry at the University of Michigan from 1962 to 1966. He then attended Elim Bible Institute in Lima, New York, along with his wife.

    In 1972, they traveled to the Philippines as missionaries, where Pastor Marvin taught at a Bible School for three years. After this, he ministered in New York for two years before moving to Guatemala, where he has ministered since 1978.

    The Byers lived in Israel for two and a half years. Currently, Pastor Marvin is the president of Hebron Ministries and pastors Hebron Church in Guatemala, Central America. He also travels to different countries along with his wife to give seminars to many who are hungry for God.
